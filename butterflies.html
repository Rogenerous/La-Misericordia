Butterflies

A striking butterfly found in Catalonia. Often mistaken for its relative, the Old World Swallowtail, the Scarce Swallowtail is slightly smaller and has a more pronounced yellow coloration with black markings. It is known for its graceful flight and can often be seen fluttering around meadows and gardens during the warmer months.

The Old World Swallowtail is one of the most recognizable butterflies in Europe. It has a distinctive yellow and black pattern with blue and red spots on its hindwings. This butterfly is often found in a variety of habitats, including gardens, meadows, and open woodlands. It is known for its strong and fast flight, making it a delight to observe.
