Olive Tree

Olive trees have played a role in the myths and religions of various civilizations for thousands of years. They are often kept low to make harvesting easier. But as you can see, this one stands in all its glory. Here at La Misericordia, our approach is not only to serve people but also to allow nature to express itself fully. That is why we let them grow tall.
