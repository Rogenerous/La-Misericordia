Mirabilis Japala

In the warm, sunlit hills of Almatret, a vibrant burst of color catches the eye. The Mirabilis jalapa, known as the Four O’Clock Flower, waits until late afternoon to unfurl its magenta blossoms, releasing a gentle fragrance into the cooling air. By morning, the blooms close again, as if keeping their beauty a secret from the midday sun. Resilient and self-seeding, this hardy plant thrives in the dry Catalonian climate, adding a splash of life to the rugged landscape.
