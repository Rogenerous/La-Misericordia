Welcome to La Misericordia
Foodforest & Homestead in Almatret

This will be a multi-year project. Progress will take a long time, but the goal is to make nature here self-sustaining, without needing help from humans. Ironically, that takes a lot of work. This website is intended as a logbook for the progress and will hopefully also grow into an overview of activities that can be done here, but also simply to show what you can encounter here.
